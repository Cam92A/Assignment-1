    My Project idea

    game compiler/organiser

    Overview

    The app will be a tool to load a list of video games onto along with information such as the average time to complete and to 100 percent. It will then help to organize and prioritise all your games into a self-generated list with a built-in calendar so that the user can plan out a way to play through their backlog of video games.

    Into the future the functionality of a forum of sorts will be added so that stories and discord along with screenshots and short clips allowing for community to help each other to complete and enjoy games.

    Motivation

    Motivation for this project is to create a companion app to help catalogue and plan or schedule a way to play through all the games that you may have collected. Today we have services like steam and other gaming platforms, which offer near instant access to thousands of current titles, with hundreds more being added regularly and often going on sale sometimes just for a few dollars, not to mention the swath of free to play titles that are available. This creates a very real dilemma of “how do I play through all these games” and this can be so overwhelming sometimes that it seems impossible to keep up. This app aims to help outline a plan and give some structure so that you can enjoy all your favourite games and share your experiences whilst doing so.

    Description

    The data base - The app would be a database or list of games added by the user, this list will be divided into completed, playing, play next and on hold sections offering a way to visualize the users backlog and to have a basic idea of how and when they will tackle their backlog.

    Syncing with other platforms - Ideally it would be able to sync with other platforms to automatically compile and update the list. I anticipate compatibility to sync lists with other services such as steam or an Xbox library to be the greatest obstacle and am only including this as a nice to have for the time being.

    Manual input - Users will have the ability to manually input their list of games and their own estimation of how long completion takes. Users will also be prompted to set parameters of completion for each title, for example an MMO might be x hours per month set indefinitely whereas a single player story-based game might be set to only complete the main line story or to 100% the game.

    Database building - Over time as more users are added I aim to build code which with the permission of users refines a scale including lower and higher time frames as well as averages as an aid to users in scheduling games to the calendar.

    Calendar function – A built in calendar which is populated with the days and blocks of time users allocate to their gaming will be used for games for scheduling game time as an aid to planning how they can clear their backlog and plan in up-and-coming titles around their free time. Part of the calendar function in unison with the users list will be the functionality for updating blocks of time on the fly in case they feel like playing a different title than what was planned. A status bar or wheel will be included for each individual title so users can take a snapshot of how far through the games they are depending on the level of completion they set in the criteria.

    Forums - A built-in forum would allow users to ask for guidance or give helpful suggestions to others and share experiences. Other information in the forums would include but are not limited to average playtime, a review page showing users comments on the games and a tips, hints, and tricks.

    Interactivity - Useful plugins to make their plans an interactive process and to give them a sense of control over how much time they spend gaming whilst having a way to track completion of all the titles they wish play. The app will also have a functionality to upload screenshots and write your own remarks for each game this will aid in picking up a game after putting it on hold for a while. This will also aim to build a sense of accomplishment with progress on the list of games and provide a good way remind you of your journey to completion when you look back at a later time.

    Tools and Technologies

    I will use the lean canvas to plan out my idea and find the relevant problem statement and identify users/buyers utilizing the agile method refine my idea in sprints. I will need a programming tool and some art design to refine the look of my app. Some research using resources on the internet will be crucial in order to establish exactly which tools will best assist me in creating my application and which are going to be either too difficult, too expensive or impractical for this task. The technological hardware tools will be a computer and a mobile phone initially with an android OS and later an Apple IOS.

    Skills Required

    I will have to learn the chosen software that I am to write as well as how to give my app the ability to link in with other platforms to help pull data such as a steam library or a console games list. I believe it will take some time to learn how to code/ build the required software. I will require a test environment for my app I anticipate I will be able to accomplish this with a pc or mobile which I already have access to so this should be easy to overcome. I will also need to be able to write my problem statement to determine if there is a need for my idea and to whom it will be targeted.

    Outcome

    Upon successful completion I will have an app that I can compile a list of games that I own which includes enough information to formulate a plan on how to play through all the games in my backlog as well as filling games into complete, in progress or coming up categories. The app will also have the ability for you to write reviews, notes, tips and tricks and upload screenshots to help immortalize your journey through completing the games you are interested or passionate about. Eventually I would like to create an additional capability to add friends and share your progress with each other and to offer community suggestions on beating a particular boss, level etc. My app will give people a way to organize their library of games and to plan how to play through them solving the problem of building up a huge backlog of games that never get completed and to be able to share this with a community.
